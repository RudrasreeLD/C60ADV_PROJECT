HI!

Camila Cabello born on March 3, 1997 is a popular singer of United States at age of only 22 years old. She started singing at her early teenager period at age of 14 years in 2012 with the team of X Factor program girls. Their group known as Fifth Harmony which she left in 2016 to start a solo journey in musical career and absolutely to make a new history on her own name. Camila Cabello born and raised in Cuba whereas she moved to American for career and for her singing journey. After working with Fifth Harmony she started singing solo albums and tracks which make a record on billboards and top charts and topped at number 1 most of the times. Camila Cabello birth name is Karla Camila Cabello Estrabao but she use only a short form of her name as “Camila Cabello”.
